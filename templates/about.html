About SlideShare Prototype

This is a locally hosted SlideShare-inspired prototype used for demonstration and testing. It showcases presentation sharing, searching, and simple account management.

Mission

Help professionals discover, share, and learn from high-quality presentations and slide decks. The project focuses on clear design, fast browsing, and accessible content discovery.

Features

  • Upload and preview presentations
  • Search and browse by tags and categories
  • User accounts with secure password storage
